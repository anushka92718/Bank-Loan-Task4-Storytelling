# Hypothesis Testing – Bank Loan Report

## 📌 Hypothesis

H0 (Null Hypothesis):  
There is no significant difference between good loans and bad loans.

H1 (Alternative Hypothesis):  
Good loans significantly outnumber bad loans.

---

## 📊 Method Used

Proportion-based analysis (percentage comparison)

Good Loans:
- Loan Status = Fully Paid + Current

Bad Loans:
- Loan Status = Charged Off

---

## 🔢 Calculation

Good Loan Percentage = (Good Loans / Total Loans) * 100  
Bad Loan Percentage = (Bad Loans / Total Loans) * 100  

(Use your SQL results here)

---

## 📈 Result

- Good Loans % = 86.2
- Bad Loans % = 13.8

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Since Good Loan Percentage is significantly higher than Bad Loan Percentage, we reject the null hypothesis.

This indicates that the majority of loans are performing well.
